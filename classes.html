Classes

Classes start every month. Textbooks are avaialable to students at no cost but may be purchased with us. Electronic copies are also available. Classes consist of lecture 4 days a week and clinical 1 day a week. A dress code is enforced on clinical days. Students should contact Regina Ayodele at 480.283.7710 for more information on dress code.
