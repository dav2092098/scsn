Classes

Classes start every month. Textbooks are provided to students at no cost for the duration of the course and may be purchased for permanent ownership. Electronic copies are also available. Classes consist of lecture 4 days a week and clinical 1-2 days a week. A dress code is enforced on clinical days. Students should contact Regina Ayodele at 602.633.4339 for more information on dress code. Scrubs may be purchased through us for ~ $15 or at the following locations and, in some cases, may be rented (if available). Gait belts, stethoscope, and other necessary equipment are also provided.

Start dates have "New" next to date time.

Our staff is experienced and dedicated to creating a supportive and challenging classroom environment.

We enroll candidates age 16 and above. There is no upper age limit for our program. Candidates may ask for a preliminary exam of basic knowledge to assess level of preparedness. For more information, call 602.935.6628.
