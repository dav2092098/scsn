Welcome

The vision of SCSN is to produce caring and sensitive Nursing Assistants who will be able to demonstrate excellence and practice ethically in the field of Nursing in the State of Arizona.

We know that finding time for school can be hard, to help accomodate busy students, we provide scheduling and financing options.

We offer:
• Day clases: Monday - Thrusday, 9AM-1PM
• Evening classes: Monday - Thursday, 5PM-9PM
• Weekend only clinical rotations
• Many open lab hours (excluding Sundays)
• Lots of personalized instruction
• Several payment plans to meet your needs1
1) Payment plans require an initial $300 installment, please call us for more details.

To qualify:
• Applicants should have a high school Diploma, GED or equivalent foreign or vocational training.
• Applicants should have the ability to communicate clearly.
• Applicants should be physically able to perform direct patient care tasks, including assisting patients with lifting, bathing, walking, and pushing an occupied wheelchair.
• Applicants must have no felony convictions in the last 5 years2.

2) Some exceptions may apply, contact us for more info.
