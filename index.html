Welcome

The vision of SCSN is to produce caring and sensitive Nursing Assistants who will be able to demonstrate excellence and practice ethically in the field of Nursing in the State of Arizona.

To qualify:
• Applicants should have a high school Diploma, GED or equivalent foreign or vocational training.
• Applicants should have the ability to communicate clearly.
• Applicants should be physically able to perform direct patient care tasks, including assiting patients with lifting, bathing, walking, and pushing an occupied wheelchair.
• 1Applicants must have no felony convictions in the last 5 years.

1Some exceptions may apply, contact us for more info.
