Welcome

The vision of SCSN is to produce caring and sensitive Nursing Assistants who will be able to demonstrate excellence and practice ethically in the field of Nursing in the State of Arizona.

We know that finding time for school can be hard, to help accomodate busy students, we offer scheduling and financing options.

• Evening classes: Monday - Thursday, 5PM-9PM
• Weekend only clinical rotations
• Payment plans available.
